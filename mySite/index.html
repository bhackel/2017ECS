What is a neural network?

An artificial neural network is a model of a biological processor, such as the brain. Neural networks can be used to find patterns in data, automate tasks, and solve problems.

Large companies such as Google and Facebook use neural networks to personalize the experience they provide. Facebook uses algorithms to target advertising, and Youtube reccommends videos by seeing what is previously viewed.

Since neural networks are best at identifying patterns or trends in data, they have are currently in use with weather forecasting, translation of languages, and text-to-speech
