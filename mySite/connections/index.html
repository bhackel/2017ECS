Audio

While researching this project, I came across a Youtube channel with neural networks recreating audio. In the videos linked in the sources, he talks about how little time it took to create the audio files compared to the thousands of hours that the artists he is trying to recreate had to practice. Karthik's topic of audio editing could have a future here with neural networks improving audio recorded by analyzing previous pieces of work. Link to Karthik's web page: Audio Editing
